You'll need to restart fmp.py with the proper path. You may need to log out and log back in for the changes to take effect.

If you started fmp.py from a terminal, then all you'll need to do is press ctrl+c type in . ~/.bashrc or . /etc/bashrc in that terminal for the changes to take effect.
